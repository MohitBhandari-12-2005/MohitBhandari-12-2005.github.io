IDEATE FOR INDIA- REGIONAL TECH CREATION BOOTCAMP

With my idea of a farmer friend robot, I was able to make in top 10 from uttar Pradesh and participated in North bootcamp of IFI in 2019. It was my firstbootcamp in my life. I explored and learnt about many new things about Electronics there, like arduino, LCD display, LEDs and Servo motor,etc. The mentors and my fellows too, helped me a lot. It was totally new for me.

IDEATE FOR INDIA- NATIONAL TECH CREATION BOOTCAMP

My teammates- Deeksha Di, Abhinav and I worked on a project E-Saheli which is a combo of sanitary napkin vending and incenerator machine. When a lady incenerates a used pad in this machine, she will get a new sanitary napkin as a reward. It would stop the throwing of used pads in open which could be a problem in many ways.
